# Early Detection and Early Intervention

---

## Slide 1: Introduction 🎉
- **What is Early Detection?**  
  Early detection refers to identifying potential health issues before they become severe.
- **What is Early Intervention?**  
  Early intervention involves taking action to treat or mitigate health issues as soon as they are identified.  
- **Importance:**  
  - Saves lives  
  - Reduces treatment costs  
  - Improves outcomes  

---

## Slide 2: The Impact of Delayed Detection ⏳
- **Consequences of Delays:**  
  - Increased severity of illness  
  - Higher medical costs  
  - Emotional and psychological strain  
- **Statistics:**  
  - 60% of diseases are more easily managed when detected early.

## Slide 4: Methods of Early Detection 🔍
- **Screenings:**  
  - Mammograms, colonoscopies, etc.  
- **Self-Exams:**  
  - Regular self-checks for lumps or unusual changes in the body  
- **Genetic Testing:**  
  - Identifying predispositions to certain diseases
